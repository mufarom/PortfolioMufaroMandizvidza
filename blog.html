Using Grids In HTML

When I began the portfolio development journey for this module, I had some prior knwoledge as to how the grids work and how to form them due to the lectures and slide notes. However, I had not realised that I only knew how to form the basic grid layout and this frustrated me as I went about my online research, finding way easier methods such as using the infamous "div" tag. I then found out that using grids creates more structure and organisation to the website, and I can now safely say I am more than comforatble with the use of grids.
